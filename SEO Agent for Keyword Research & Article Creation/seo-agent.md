## SEO Agent for Keyword Research & Article Creation
An end-to-end n8n automation that takes a seed keyword from Google Sheets, uses Google Autocomplete and the Exa API to research relevant content, then leverages Nvidia Nemotron and Deepseek r1 to generate SEO-optimized publish-ready articles and automatically saves all keywords and final articles back to Google Sheets for seamless content management.
