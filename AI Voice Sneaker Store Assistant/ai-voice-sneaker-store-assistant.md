## AI Voice Sneaker Store Assistant

A voice-powered inventory lookup system where customers call an AI agent (built on VAPI), ask about sneaker availability in real-time from a Google Sheets database, and automatically receive a branded confirmation or recommendation email based on the outcome of the call.
