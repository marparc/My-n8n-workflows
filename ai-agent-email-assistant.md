## AI Agent Email Assistant
An n8n workflow that acts as an intelligent email assistant. When a chat message is received requesting to send an email to a specific customer by name, an AI Agent takes over — querying a Google Sheets customer database to look up the recipient's email address, then crafting and sending a personalized email automatically. No manual lookup needed; just tell it who to email and what to say.
