## Daily Motivational Quote Generator

An n8n workflow that automatically generates and delivers a fresh AI-written motivational quote to a curated list of recipients every day. A schedule trigger runs the workflow daily, prompts an AI model to generate a unique quote, pulls a recipient list from a connected Google Sheets database, and sends each person a personalized email.
